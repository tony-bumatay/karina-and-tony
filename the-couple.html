KARINA AND TONY

- ABOUT THE GROOM -

Tony lives in Brighton, MA but his hometown is Palos Verdes, CA, home to some of the best breakfast burritos and ice cream you’ll ever have - shout out to Phanny’s and Handel's! After graduating from Washington University in STL, he moved to Boston and started living life on the East Coast, immersing himself in a perfect blend of camp and college friends. Tony regularly scores deals on furniture for his friends, through his job as a software developer at Wayfair. When he’s not on a late night 2am coding bender, Tony loves playing frisbee, skiing, cooking tacos for his friends, or playing board games. Also, Tony is the most reliable airport pick up/drop off ride you’ll ever find. And if you need help moving across the country, he’s got your back!

- Written by the bride to be, Karina

- ABOUT THE BRIDE -

Karina lived in southern California in her early childhood until her family moved back to Connecticut when she was five. Thankfully, annual summer trips back to Balboa helped her remain a Californian at heart! One of the greatest things about Karina is her love of Mexican food; We had a blast sampling all her favorite San Diego taco spots during an extended stay with Karsten and Brie last spring! In Boston, Karina often serves as the game night, baking party, and ski trip catalyst among our group of friends. Unfortunately, we can’t watch TV together because Karina’s two primary TV genres are horror (YIKES) and trash teen mystery (Nancy Drew), but that’s probably for the better. I’m excited that I've found my perfect Shotgun Rider for all the future adventures in Big Betty.

- Written by the groom to be, Tony

MEET BETTY

Betty or “Big Betty & Co” is Tony’s Ford E350 15 passenger van. Tony bought Betty in the fall of 2019 with the immediate plan of carting his friends to the mountains for fun adventures, and a long term plan of maybe one day building it out. For almost 2 years, Betty took trips all over New England during the summer and winter, and many memories were made, hiking, skiing, swimming, and more.

BETTY'S BUILD OUT

In the summer of 2020, Tony & Katie started building out the van, converting it into a livable “ski chateau”. From their home in Palos Verdes, they began with the initial framing, insulation, and wiring. Eventually, Tony drove Betty back to Boston where he and Karina have been working on it ever since. Neither of them really know anything about building, so it involves a lot of youtube and multiple trips to Home Depot after buying the wrong product 3 times in a row.
