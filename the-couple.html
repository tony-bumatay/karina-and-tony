- ABOUT THE GROOM -

Tony lives in Brighton, MA but his hometown is Palos Verdes, CA, home to some of the best breakfast burritos and ice cream you’ll ever have - shout out to Phanny’s and Handles! Besides scoring deals on furniture for his friends, Tony’s is a geeky software developer at Wayfair who breaks all the stereotypes. When he’s not on a late night 2am coding bender, Tony is always hiking, skiing, kayaking, or working on building out our livable van “Betty”. His love for constant adventure is one of my favorite things about him. - Written by the bride to be

MEET BETTY

Betty or “Big Betty & Co” is Tony’s Ford E350 15 passenger van. Tony bought Betty in the fall of 2019 with the immediate plan of carting his friends to the mountains for fun adventures, and a long term plan of maybe one day building it out. For almost 2 years, Betty took trips all over New England during the summer and winter, and many memories were made, hiking, skiing, swimming, and more.

BETTY'S BUILD OUT

In the summer of 2020, Tony & Katie started building out the van, converting it into a livable “ski chateau”. From their home in Palos Verdes, they began with the initial framing, insulation, and wiring. Eventually, Tony drove Betty back to Boston where he and Karina have been working on it ever since. Neither of them really know anything about building, so it involves a lot of youtube and multiple trips to Home Depot after buying the wrong product 3 times in a row.
