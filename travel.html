What is Camp Ogontz?

Formerly a girls camp, Ogontz, is a 350 acre private rustic resort, nestled deep within the forests of New Hampshire's White Mountains. The camp rests above its own lake and is complete with mountain views, streams, waterfalls and miles of wooded hiking trails. On property activities include swimming, canoeing, kayaking, lawn games, woodburning hot tubs and bonfires. Additionally, the facilities include a provisions store and café, tennis courts, volleyball courts, and a private boat house. If you want to arrive and stay at Ogontz for the entirety of the weekend, there is plenty to do and ample space to gather with loved ones. Think… family camp!

ACCOMMODATIONS

CAMP OGONTZ - 14 Ogontz Road, Lyman NH 03585

Rustic accommodations are available on the Ogontz property. We have booked the entire resort, meaning the only visitors on property will be guests of our wedding. We hope that all our guests consider staying on property to be in the heart of the festivities. These accommodations are comparable to a “family camp” environment. While cabins have full mattresses (not bunks), they are rustic. Bedding and towels are provided and cabins can fit up to 4 people. There are two large communal bathroom halls on the property, along with a few other bathrooms located around the property. Temperatures in October are variable and unpredictable. If you are driving or have space in your luggage, we encourage guests to bring an additional personal towel and a blanket or sleeping bag.

HAMPTON INN LITTLETON - 580 Meadow St, Littleton, NH 03561

This standard Hampton Inn is located a short 15 minute drive from Camp Ogontz near the quaint town of Littleton, NH. While this hotel does not do room blocks, we were able to secure a set price for our guests. Littleton is a popular destination for tourists in the fall and the weekend of our wedding will be peak timing for fall foliage. If you would like to stay at the Hampton Inn or any other hotel/bed and breakfast in the area, we recommend you book your room no later than March 2022. Other recommended accommodations in the area are the Sugar Hill Inn, Franconia Inn, and Best Western White Mountain Inn. Please contact Carolyn Bumatay or Meridee Olsen with any inquiries about accommodations as they will be happy to help you understand the area!

NEARBY POINTS OF INTEREST

Franconia Notch

Franconia Notch is a highly trafficed and loved area of New England, perfect hiking and outdoor exploration. Check out All Trails to find a hike that works for you and your group.

Littleton, NH

Otherwise known as “The Glad Town”, this adorable New England town is the origin of the storybook character, Pollyanna. It includes coffee shops, local ice cream spots, fashion boutiques, and an iconic covered bridge, all running parallel to a gorgeous, lively river.

Kancamagus Highway

A 34.5 mile scenic drive along NH's Rt. 112, The Kancamagus Highway is known as one of the best fall foliage viewing areas in the country. The Byway takes you through the White Mountain National Forest with breathtaking views of the White Mountains, the Swift River, Sabbaday Falls, Lower Falls and Rocky Gorge. It also runs through Lincoln, NH.

Lincoln, NH

Lincoln is home to Loon ski resort, one of Tony and Karina's favorite places to ski in the Northeast. During warmer months, you can visit Flume Gorge or take a tour to see local wildlife. Lincoln is another small New England town with plenty to see, do, and explore.
