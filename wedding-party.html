KARINA'S WEDDING PARTY

KARSTEN OLSEN

- BROTHER -

Karsten is Karina’s dear big brother. As her only sibling, they share a love of California, a silly humor, and a joy for going on neighborhood runs. Kind, unceasingly supportive, and always loyal, Karsten is truly the best big brother you could ask for. He's by far the funniest one in the family and is always making everyone laugh. From March-June of 2020, Karina and Tony were able to live with Brie and Karsten in San Diego for about eight weeks. This invaluable and unexpected time together was truly a gift and it allowed Karina and Karsten to hang out in a way they haven’t done since childhood. Plus, Karsten got a first hand look at some of Karina and Tony’s earliest whisperings of marriage, casually delivering loving side eyes to Tony. Karina loves watching Karsten be a new dad and is eternally grateful to have a brother as cool, calm, and collected as Karsten.

BRIE OLSEN

- SISTER-IN-LAW -

Karina and Brie’s friendship started in college when Brie was Karina’s RA. After graduation, Karina lived with Brie and Karsten for about nine months and Brie quickly became the big sister that Karina never had. Now, Brie is an expert at frequent, quick phone calls to say hi and check in. She is “go with the flow”, flexible, and always willing to host friends and family at their home in San Diego. The best thing about Brie is that she really lives what it means to put family first. She’s always plotting for the next Olsen family gathering. Plus, her commitment to getting Karsten to play board games with the family has made her and Karina an unstoppable duo. As a new mama, she’s an expert at thrifting, DIY projects, and constantly demonstrating how to be graceful, especially when her daughter Shae is flinging food all over the kitchen.

KATY MCALONEY

- CHILDHOOD BFF -

Katy is Karina’s oldest friend of the bunch and they met at a preschool Meet & Greet before they could talk. After Karina’s family moved away from CA, the two were only able to reunite a couple times a year throughout their entire childhood. With summers spent on Balboa, scootering around on Razors and getting ice cream from Dad’s Ice Cream Shop, most of their 27 years of friendship has been long distance! It wasn’t until college that they could hang out every day and even be roommates for a semester. Now, Katy and Karina both live in Boston and their lifelong friendship is still a special treasure. They get to casually hang out on the weekends, cook dinners together, or head north for a weekend ski trip. You can bet they’ve both got their matching broken heart “best-friend” necklaces buried deep in a dresser drawer somewhere.

ERICA DILELLO

- HIGH SCHOOL OG -

Erica is an original homie from Wilton, CT, ever since the 3rd grade. Fun fact: it was a delirious 4am trip to the Danbury mall on Black Friday in highschool which led to Erica’s originating of the term “Ladies”, a name which Karina and her high school friends frequently call themselves. Since the days of girl scouts, soccer practice, and routine sleepovers, Erica has been a best friend through it all. Now, she’s living in Colorado, crushing grad school at Vassar, excelling in the field of green building design, and living life to the fullest. She does epic adventures like getting her avalanche training certificate for backcountry skiing on the slopes or hiking 14ers with her dogs and fiance. Beyond that, Erica is one of the most genuine and silliest people you’ll ever meet. It would be remiss to end this bio without a quick note saying... “Can I get a ladiessss?!?”

WENDY WALCOFF

- FIRST CO, FIRST LOVE -

Truth be told about Wendy, she was actually Tony’s friend first. But after their first summer as co-counselors together in 2012, Karina and Wendy became inseparable adventure buddies. From skiing all over the country, traveling internationally, hiking in the PNW, and spending the last 4 New Year’s Eves together, these two always find themselves back in planning mode for the next big adventure. Learning the ins and outs of the entire PNW, Wendy has such a love for learning, growth and exploration. You won’t find anyone with a bigger heart, whether it's sharing her camping gear or learning how to be a better member of her communnity. Wendy defines what it means to be a reliable and trustworthy friend.

GEORGIA MAE HARRISON

- CAMP & COLLEGE BESTIE -

No matter where life takes them, Georgia and Karina always find themselves pondering and learning about the same big ideas. From plotting as college roommates about who they might marry to learning about how to “do adulthood”, Georgia is the friend who will spend hours with you on the phone, listening to your every crazy idea or inkling, and celebrating your hopes and dreams. As a recent mom to the most adorable twin girls, Sadie Jane and Henley, Georgia is constantly putting her loved ones first. These days, you’ll find her in the Midwest, teaching Karina from afar all about the ins and outs of motherhood, while cheering Karina on through the entire wedding planning process.

LAUREN LITTELL

- BOSTON ROOMMATE -

Fashionista, Lauren Littell, can take almost all the credit (along with Meridee) for Karina’s fashion knowledge. Nobody knows Karina’s wardrobe better than Lauren and as current roommates in Boston, she is Karina’s #1 consultant for almost any new purchase. Lauren is also a true “foodie”, so the two women are always analyzing and critiquing their latest meal. A weekend with Lauren and Karina includes blasting Maggie Rogers in the car, a long ride on their new road bikes, and exploring around for the yummiest, local food they can find. But deeper than weekend plans and Friday night shenanigans, Lauren’s friendship has been a rock to Karina through their years in college and young adulthood. As both employees of The Mother Church, they cherish their time living in Boston together and serving their loved & shared religion.
