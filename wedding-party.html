KARINA'S WEDDING PARTY

KARSTEN OLSEN - MAN OF HONOR

- BROTHER -

Karsten is Karina’s dear big brother. As her only sibling, they share a love of California, a silly and goofy humor, and a joy for going on neighborhood runs. Kind, unceasingly supportive, and always loyal, Karsten is truly the best big brother you could ask for. From March-June of 2020, Karina and Tony were able to live with Brie and Karsten in San Diego for about eight weeks. This invaluable and unexpected time together was truly a gift and it allowed Karina and Karsten to hang out in a way they haven’t done since childhood. Plus, Karsten got a first hand look at some of Karina and Tony’s earliest whisperings of marriage, delivering loving side eyes to Tony as he noticed that Tony wasn’t planning on going anywhere. Karina loves watching Karsten be a new dad and is eternally grateful to have a brother as cool, calm, and collected as Karsten.

BRIE OLSEN

- SISTER-IN-LAW -

Karina and Brie’s friendship started in college when Brie was Karina’s Resident Advisor. After graduation, Karina lived with Brie and Karsten for about nine months and Brie quickly became the big sister that Karina never had. Now, Brie is an expert at frequent, quick phone calls to say hi and check in. She is “go with the flow”, flexible, and always willing to host friends and family at their home in San Diego. The best thing about Brie is that she really lives what it means to put family first. She’s always plotting for the next Olsen family gathering. Plus, her commitment to getting Karsten to play board games with the family has made her and Karina an unstoppable duo. As a new mama, she’s an expert at thrifting, DIY projects, and constantly demonstrating how to be graceful, especially when Shae is flinging food across the kitchen.

KATY MCALONEY

- CHILDHOOD BFF -

Katy is Karina’s oldest friend of the bunch and they met at Clairbourn Preschool before they could talk. After Karina’s family moved away from CA, the two were only able to reunite a couple times a year throughout their entire childhood. With summers spent on Balboa, scootering around on Razors and getting ice cream from Dad’s Ice Cream Shop, most of their 27 years of friendship has been long distance! It wasn’t until college that they were able to hang out every day and even be roommates for a semester. Now, Katy and Karina both live in Boston and their lifelong friendship is still a special treasure to them both. They get to casually hang out on the weekends, cook dinners together, or head north for a weekend ski trip. You can bet they’ve got their matching broken heart “best-friend” necklaces buried deep in a dresser drawer somewhere.

ERICA DILELLO

- HIGH SCHOOL OG -

Erica is an OG homie from Wilton, CT, ever since the 3rd grade. Fun fact: it was a delirious 4am trip to the Danbury mall on Black Friday in highschool which led to Erica’s originating of the term “Ladies”, a name which Karina and her high school friends frequently call themselves. Since the days of girl scouts, soccer practice, and routine sleepovers, Erica has been a best friend through it all. Now, she’s living in Colorado, crushing graduate school, excelling in the field of green-building design and policy, and living life to the fullest. She sets out on epic adventures, like getting her avalanche training certificate for backcountry skiing or hiking 14-ers with her dogs and fiance. Beyond that, Erica is one of the most genuine and silliest people you’ll ever meet. It would be remiss to end this bio without a quick note saying... “Can I get a ladiessss?!?”

WENDY WALCOFF

- FIRST CO, FIRST LOVE -

Truth be told about Wendy, she was actually Tony’s friend first. But after their first summer as co-counselors together at Camp Newfound in 2012, Karina and Wendy became inseparable adventure buddies. From skiing all over the country, traveling internationally, hiking in the PNW, and spending the last four New Year’s Eves together, the two always find themselves back in planning mode for the next big trip. Whether it's hiking, sailing, kayaking, or biking, Wendy's always down. When she’s not adventuring, Wendy is either mentoring high school and elementary school students, studying hard at the University of Washington, planning a themed dance party, or selflessly sharing her camping gear with others. She has the biggest heart and a love for learning. It’s now a decade after Karina and Wendy’s first year on Newfound staff together as co-counselors and their friendship is stronger than ever as they cheerfully reminisce, “first co, first love!”

GEORGIA MAE HARRISON

- CAMP & COLLEGE BESTIE -

No matter where life takes them, Georgia and Karina always find themselves pondering and learning about the same big ideas. From plotting as college roommates about relationships to learning how to “do adulthood”, Georgia is the friend who will spend hours with you on the phone, listening to your every crazy idea or inkling, and celebrating your hopes and dreams. The two share a love for chocolate, Maine, and waterskiing, after their many summers at camp together. Georgia keeps Karina up to date with the latest beauty products and has been a constant cheerleader throughout the entire wedding planning process. As a recent mom to the most adorable twin girls, Sadie Jane and Henley, Georgia is always putting her loved ones first. These days, you’ll find her in the Midwest, teaching Karina from afar about the joys and surprises of motherhood and homeownership, all while helping her husband with their family business, Fort Worth Running Company.

LAUREN LITTELL

- BOSTON ROOMMATE -

Fashionista, Lauren Littell, can take almost all the credit (along with Meridee) for Karina’s fashion knowledge. Nobody knows Karina’s wardrobe better than Lauren and as current roommates in Boston, she is Karina’s #1 consultant for almost any new purchase. Lauren is also a true “foodie”, so she and Karina are always analyzing and critiquing their latest meal. A weekend with Lauren and Karina includes blasting Maggie Rogers in the car, a long ride on their new road bikes, and hunting around for the yummiest, local food they can find. But deeper than weekend plans and Friday night shenanigans, Lauren’s friendship has been a rock to Karina through their years in college and young adulthood. As both employees of The Mother Church, they cherish their time living in Boston together and serving their loved & shared religion. Plus, just one or two nudges from Lauren set the Karina & Tony relationship buds into full bloom!

TONY'S WEDDING PARTY

KATIE BUMATAY - BEST WOMAN

- SISTER -

Tony and Katie share a special sibling bond, forged by the childhood dinner cycle of baked chicken and rice, leftover chicken and bean burritos, and chicken broth ramen. A typical childhood summer consisted of trips to Yosemite and the family cottage with all the cousins in Harpswell, Maine. In adulthood, the adventures continued with an expanded playground, but the same childlike joy and love for spending quality time together in the outdoors. More recently, Tony and Katie’s plot to get their parents excited about skiing has worked to perfection and Randy and Carolyn logged 50+ days on the slopes last year. Katie is fearless and dedicated when it comes to all things adventure, whether it’s skiing double black diamonds, trail running up a mountain, or flying cross-country for a weekend in Baxter State Park. As the sibling adventures began including Karina, it’s been a blessing that Katie and Karina have such a close relationship of their own. Official sisterhood is just around the corner!

ANDREW PARSONS

- CO DANCING DESSERT DUDE -

Andrew is a true renaissance man: singer, photographer, painter, drawer, dancer, and chef. They just don’t make men the way Andrew was made anymore. Tony and Andrew became friends as counselors at Owatonna and then really became besties once they both moved to Boston around the same time in fall of 2017. Andrew is liable to FaceTime you at 5pm and ask if you want him to come over to YOUR apartment and cook dinner or bake goodies together. Except, when he’s the professional cook of the duo, “together” usually just means riffing off each other’s goonery while he does all the actual cooking. That pattern led to the sadly short-lived formation of "The Dancing Dessert Dudes" - an Instagram account where Andrew and Tony made desserts and shamelessly danced around the kitchen. Andrew is wonderfully spontaneous, a true “yes man” when convincing him to join in on a crazy idea, and is one of the nicest guys you’ll ever meet.

NIREK SHARMA

- HUNNEWELL BUNKMATE -

It was mac & cheese Mondays, burning the midnight oil at SFLT (Second Floor Long Tables in the Olin Library, for the uninitiated), and the WashU Ultimate Frisbee team that started Tony and Nirek’s friendship. Their spring break road trip in 2017 alluded to the type of adventures that would fill their future: a distinct lack of planning, a certain optimism about their capabilities, and a habit of forgoing proper sleep for unforgettable memories. Fast forward to 2019, Nirek moved to Boston to become roommates with Tony, Matt, and Van in their apartment, affectionately known as “Beluga Heights”. Most people wouldn’t opt to sleep in a bunk bed as an adult with a real income... but, Nirek and Tony aren’t most people and their friendship only grew stronger after voluntarily deciding to become bunkmates. Nirek is the ultimate hype-man, a living defintion of loyalty, and he'll pump you up for the tinest victories. He's the kind of friend who will pick your girlfriend up from the airport at 5am if you’re not home to do it yourself. He has an infectious proclivity for buckets, is thoughtful and kind, and best of all, ladies, he is single!

CODY VEIDELIS

- CHILDHOOD CAMP FRIEND -

Cody and Tony are life long summer camp friends dating all the way back to Cabin 9 in 2006 when Cody showed up with an infamous mullet hair-do and tie dye bedding. Since then, they have spent nine summers together at Camp Owatonna. From big adventures, like their Maine Island trail JC sea kayaking trip and Allagash Wilderness Waterway canoe trip, to the daily shenanigans and 5am adirondack showers before Sunday cabin cleanup, summers in Maine with Cody were always one of Tony’s favorite parts of the year. For their final summer together in 2016, they were co-counselors leading the Counselor in Training program in what was one of the most demanding and rewarding summers of them all. When Cody and Cassidy became roommates with Tony and Nirek in Boston, Tony’s worlds collided in the best way. Cody became the Among Us ringleader and the whole Boston friend group became hooked! As a captivating and hilarious story teller, nobody can make Tony squeal with laughter like Cody can. It was such a special time to live with Cody and Cass before they moved out West to pursue the next chapter of their lives.

NATE WATERS

-INTERNATIONAL SWIMSUIT MODEL-

Nate and Tony were both long-time campers at Owatonna, but didn’t truly become great friends until they were on staff together. Nate and Jordan Anderson taught Tony the art of the DOFF in the summer of 2013 - packing maximum adventure and activity into their days off together. Then, a trip to Spain with Karina and Wendy sealed the deal on a lifelong friendship for Nate and Tony, especially after buying matching short-short swimtrunks. When he's not waterskiing and gettin' buoys, Nate might be confused for a professional homemade pasta and pizza extraordinaire. He is the perfect foil to Tony's lackluster “long distance keeping in touch” skills; frequently calling Tony while driving in Seattle traffic or walking Mischa, he's always ready to catch up or ask for the Round Report on Tony and Kenny's latest Maple Hill disc golf excursion. A quick call and Nate's listening ear makes the distance disappear and with any luck, Tony and Nate won’t be on opposite coasts for too much longer.

KENNY SYDNESS

- FELLOW GREEN -

Although Kenny couldn’t quite recruit Tony to Principia College, he did much better the second time around, convincing Tony to move to Boston after college. During Tony’s senior year, a weekend trip full of rugby, ultimate frisbee in the snow, and bopping around different parts of Boston really sealed the deal. After Tony officially moved to Brighton, they maintained the active pace as the “most single dudes in Boston” spending most weekends exploring the Northeast, road tripping to Florida, and everything in between. Kenny’s friendship was one of the main factors that made Tony’s move to Boston so seamless. Kenny was also a founding member of the Rose Buds - a group of dedicated Bachelor fans that convened weekly to watch the Bachelor and inadvertently received noise complaints from the Strathmore neighbors. Let’s be honest, it was Crystal’s fault. 4+ years later, Kenny and Tony are now roommates and planning their 2022 weddings in parallel!

SACHITH SIRIWARDANE

The laundry list of freshman year shenanigans was long, but if there was a noise complaint on Keonig 1 (there were many) the duo was likely to be involved. With dorms across the hall from one another, the two instantly became friends. Bierstadt, with Sach in the summer!
