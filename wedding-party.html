KARINA'S WEDDING PARTY

KARSTEN OLSEN - MAN OF HONOR

- BROTHER -

Karsten is Karina’s dear big brother. As her only sibling, they share a love of California, a silly and goofy humor, and a joy for going on neighborhood runs. Kind, unceasingly supportive, and always loyal, Karsten is truly the best big brother you could ask for. From March-June of 2020, Karina and Tony were able to live with Brie and Karsten in San Diego for about 8 weeks. This invaluable and unexpected time together was truly a gift and it allowed Karina and Karsten to hang out in a way they haven’t done since childhood. Plus, Karsten got a first hand look at some of Karina and Tony’s earliest whisperings of marriage, delivering loving side eyes to Tony as he noticed Tony wasn’t planning on going anywhere. Karina loves watching Karsten be a new dad and is eternally grateful to have a brother as cool, calm, and collected as Karsten.

BRIE OLSEN

- SISTER-IN-LAW -

Karina and Brie’s friendship started in college when Brie was Karina’s RA. After graduation, Karina lived with Brie and Karsten for about nine months and Brie quickly became the big sister that Karina never had. Now, Brie is an expert at frequent, quick phone calls to say hi and check in. She is “go with the flow”, flexible, and always willing to host friends and family at their home in San Diego. The best thing about Brie is that she really lives what it means to put family first. She’s always plotting for the next Olsen family gathering. Plus, her commitment to getting Karsten to play board games with the family has made her and Karina an unstoppable duo. As a new mama, she’s an expert at thrifting, DIY projects, and constantly demonstrating how to be graceful, especially when her daughter Shae is flinging food all over the kitchen.

KATY MCALONEY

- CHILDHOOD BFF -

Katy is Karina’s oldest friend of the bunch and they met at a Clairbourn Preschool Meet & Greet before they could talk. After Karina’s family moved away from CA, the two were only able to reunite a couple times a year throughout their entire childhood. With summers spent on Balboa together, scootering around on Razors and getting ice cream from Dad’s Ice Cream Shop, most of their 27 years of friendship has been long distance! It wasn’t until college that they were able to hang out every day and even be roommates for a semester. Now, Katy and Karina both live in Boston and their lifelong friendship is still a special treasure to them both. They get to casually hang out on the weekends, cook dinners together, or head north for a weekend ski trip. You can bet they’ve both got their matching broken heart “best-friend” necklaces buried deep in a dresser drawer somewhere.

ERICA DILELLO

- HIGH SCHOOL OG -

Erica is an OG homie from Wilton, CT, ever since the 3rd grade. Fun fact: it was a delirious 4am trip to the Danbury mall on Black Friday in highschool which led to Erica’s originating of the term “Ladies”, a name which Karina and her high school friends frequently call themselves. Since the days of girl scouts, soccer practice, and routine sleepovers, Erica has been a best friend through it all. Now, she’s living in Colorado, crushing grad school at Vassar, excelling in the field of green building design, and living life to the fullest. She does epic adventures like getting her avalanche training certificate for backcountry skiing on the slopes or hiking 14-ers with her dogs and fiance. Beyond that, Erica is one of the most genuine and silliest people you’ll ever meet. It would be remiss to end this bio without a quick note saying... “Can I get a ladiessss?!?”

WENDY WALCOFF

- FIRST CO, FIRST LOVE -

Truth be told about Wendy, she was actually Tony’s friend first. But after their first summer as co-counselors together at Camp Newfound in 2012, Karina and Wendy became inseparable adventure buddies. From skiing all over the country, traveling internationally, hiking in the PNW, and spending the last four New Year’s Eves together, the two always find themselves back in planning mode for the next big trip. Whether it's hiking, sailing, kayaking, or biking, Wendy does it all. When she’s not adventuring, Wendy is either mentoring high school and elementary school students, studying hard at the University of Washington, planning a themed dance party, or selflessly sharing her camping gear with others. She has the biggest heart and a love for learning. It’s now a decade after Karina and Wendy’s first year on Newfound staff together as co-counselors and their friendship is stronger than ever as they cheerfully reminisce, “first co, first love!”

GEORGIA MAE HARRISON

- CAMP & COLLEGE BESTIE -

No matter where life takes them, Georgia and Karina always find themselves pondering and learning about the same big ideas. From plotting as college roommates about relationships to learning how to “do adulthood”, Georgia is the friend who will spend hours with you on the phone, listening to your every crazy idea or inkling, and celebrating your hopes and dreams. The two share a love for chocolate, Maine, and waterskiing, after their many summers at camp together. Georgia keeps Karina up to date with the latest beauty products and has been a constant cheerleader throughout the entire wedding planning process. As a recent mom to the most adorable twin girls, Sadie Jane and Henley, Georgia is always putting her loved ones first. These days, you’ll find her in the Midwest, teaching Karina from afar about the joys and surprises of motherhood, all while helping her husband with their family business, Fortworth Running Company.

LAUREN LITTELL

- BOSTON ROOMMATE -

Fashionista, Lauren Littell, can take almost all the credit (along with Meridee) for Karina’s fashion knowledge. Nobody knows Karina’s wardrobe better than Lauren and as current roommates in Boston, she is Karina’s #1 consultant for almost any new purchase. Lauren is also a true “foodie”, so she and Karina are always analyzing and critiquing their latest meal. A weekend with Lauren and Karina includes blasting Maggie Rogers in the car, a long ride on their new road bikes, and hunting around for the yummiest, local food they can find. But deeper than weekend plans and Friday night shenanigans, Lauren’s friendship has been a rock to Karina through their years in college and young adulthood. As both employees of The Mother Church, they cherish their time living in Boston together and serving their loved & shared religion. Plus, just one or two nudges from Lauren set the Karina & Tony relationship buds into full bloom!

TONY'S WEDDING PARTY

KATIE BUMATAY - BEST WOMAN

- SISTER -

Tony and Katie share a special sibling bond, forged by the childhood dinner cycle of baked chicken and rice, leftover chicken and bean burritos, and chicken broth ramen. A typical childhood summer consisted of hiking and riding bikes all over the Yosemite valley together, visiting the family cottage with all the cousins in Harpswell, Maine, and spending time at Camps Newfound and Owatonna together. Fast forward to adulthood and the adventures continued with an expanded playground, but the same childlike joy and love for spending quality time together in the outdoors. More recently, Tony and Katie’s plot to get their parents excited about skiing has somehow worked better than they could have ever imagined. Randy logged 50+ days on the slopes last winter with Carolyn not far behind. Katie is fearless and dedicated when it comes to all things adventure, whether it’s skiing double black diamonds, trail running up a mountain, or flying across the country for a weekend in Baxter State Park. As the sibling adventures began including Karina, it’s been a blessing that Katie and Karina have such a close relationship of their own. Official sisterhood is just around the corner!

ANDREW PARSONS

- CO DANCING DESSERT DUDE -

Andrew is a TRUE renaissance man. Singer, photographer, painter, artist, dancer, and chef. They just don’t make men the way Andrew was made anymore. Tony and Andrew became friends as counselors at Owatonna and then really became besties once they both moved to Boston around the same time in fall of 2017. Need a recommendation for a moving company? Don’t ask Andrew. When he first moved to Boston he crashed on an air mattress in Tony’s room and was constantly baking delicious goodies to appease his feeling of imposing for too long. When he isn't already crashing on your couch, he’s liable to FaceTime you at 5pm and ask if you want him to come over to YOUR apartment and cook dinner or bake goodies together. Except, when he’s the professional cook of the duo, “together” usually just means riffing off each other’s goonery while he does all the actual cooking. Jokes aside, the joy of the early days living in Boston set the stage and the two never looked back. Andrew is wonderfully spontaneous, a true “yes man” when it comes to convincing him to join in on a crazy idea, and is one of the nicest people you’ll ever meet.

NIREK SHARMA

- HUNNEWELL BUNKMATE -

It was mac & cheese Mondays, burning the midnight oil at SFLT (Second Floor Long Tables in the Olin Library, for the uninitiated), and the WashU Ultimate Frisbee team that led to instant friendship between Tony and Nirek. Their spring break road trip in 2017 alluded to the type of adventures that would fill their future: a distinct lack of planning, a certain optimism about their own abilities, and a habit of forgoing proper sleep for unforgettable memories. Fast forward to 2019, Nirek moved to Boston to become roommates with Tony, Matt, and Van in their apartment, affectionately known as “Beluga Heights”. Most people wouldn’t opt to live in a bunk bed as an adult with a real income. Most friendships would implode after being bunkmates for 12 months. But, Nirek and Tony aren’t most people and their friendship only grew stronger. Watching 6am inspirational Kobe videos together and falling asleep to Chris Bentchetler ski films were staples of the bunkroom experience. Nirek is the kind of friend who will pick your girlfriend up from the airport at 5am on a work day when you’re not home to do it yourself. He has an infectious proclivity for buckets, is thoughtful, loving, and kind, and best of all, ladies, he is single!
