FAQs

Travel

What is the closest airport?

Boston Logan Airport (BOS) is the most convenient airport to fly into for the weekend for distance and price. It is a 2.5 hour drive from BOS to Ogontz. Both Manchester-Boston Regional Airport (MHT) and Burlington International Airport (BTV) are just under 2 hours from Ogontz, though they may be more expensive.

Will any transportation or shuttle service be provided?

Unfortunately, transporation or shuttle services will not be offered.

Will there be parking?

Ogontz has a large parking lot on property which is available for all wedding guests to use throughout the weekend. Whether you are staying on Ogontz property or at a local hotel, parking will be available as you attend the wedding activities. If you're staying in any of the on-venue lodging options, please be prepared to unload all of your belongings at your cabin and then park in a central parking lot.

What should I do if I can’t attend?

You will most definitely be missed! Please RSVP here as soon as possible. If your availability changes down the road and you are able to attend, please get in touch with Karina and Tony as soon as possible so they can maintain an accurate headcount.

Ceremony and Reception

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

The wedding ceremony will be located outside on a lawn in the center of camp. Please expect to walk on grass. The reception will take place indoors in the Ogontz lodge, which has central air and heating.

What is the wedding ceremony & reception dress code?

The dress code for the wedding ceremony and reception is formal or cocktail attire. This includes cocktail dresses, dressy jumpsuits, suits and/or jackets & slacks. Please take into consideration that the wedding ceremony will be on a grassy lawn when selecting your choice of footwear.

Are children welcome?

Of course! Children are most welcome to attend all events throughout the weekend. However, please note that no child care will be provided. If you have young and squirmy ones, please be prepared to step out from the ceremony if necessary.

Will alcohol be served at the wedding?

In accordance with both Karina and Tony's religious & lifestyle choices, this will be a dry wedding. Nonetheless, come ready to socialize and hit the dance floor!

Logistics

What is the weather at that time of year?

The first weekend in October typically marks the start of peak fall foliage season in New Hampshire. Temperatures can range from mid 50s - low 70s during the day and drop into the 40s at night. We're hoping for sunshine and crisp leaf-peeping weather, but colder temps and rain are a possibility. Please bring your favorite sweater and a of couple jackets. Additionally, please check the forecast as we get closer to the event. As Tony likes to say: there's no such thing as bad weather - only bad gear.

What is the dress code for Saturday and Monday?

The dress code for the Welcome Dinner is casual or business casual. Karina and Tony will probably be wearing a sundress and khakis and a collared shirt. The dress code for the Farewell Breakfast is casual. Feel free to come in your PJs or your travel attire.

What should I pack?

Overall, pack like you are going to attend family camp! Here are some suggestions to get you started:

  • Your wedding attire
  • Casual dress or outfit for welcome dinner
  • 1-2 athletic outfits
  • 1-2 jackets/sweatshirts
  • Sneakers
  • Bathing suit for those brave enough
  • Extra towel
  • Extra blankets/sleeping bag - bedding is provided in all the cabins on Ogontz property, but if it happens to be a particularly cold evening, having an extra blanket or sleeping bag will make you a happy camper.
  • Some may enjoy having a small packable mirror to aid in the getting ready process. There are mirrors throughout camp, but they are mostly located in the communal bathroom areas.

What is the game Sock Attack, which will be taking place on Saturday afternoon?

Sock Attack is on of Karina and Tony’s favorite camp field game. Plan to wear sneakers and athletic clothes. This is a game for ALL AGES and they hope everyone will enjoy playing! If not, you are welcome to watch or socialize on the sidelines.

What meals are provided for me during my stay, other than the welcome dinner, reception dinner, and farewell breakfast?

For those staying on Ogontz property, a light breakfast will be provided Sunday morning. For lunch on Sunday, guests will need to buy their own meal from Ogontz Provisions & Cafe or venture off property to find some local bites in Littleton.
