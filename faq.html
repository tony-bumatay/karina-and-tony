Q: Will there be parking?

A: Yep! There is an abundance of parking spots at Ogontz for guests who are staying in accomodations on the venue as well as guests who are staying off the venue and are parking for the day. If you're staying in any of the on-venue lodging options please be prepared to unload all of your belongings at your cabin and park in a central parking lot.

Q: What is the dress code?

A: Cocktail Attire. This includes cocktail dresses, dressy jumpsuits, suits and/or jackets & slacks. Please take into consideration that the wedding ceremony will be on a grassy lawn when selecting your choice of footwear.

Q: Are children welcome?

A: Of course! Children are most welcome to attend all events throughout the weekend. However, please do note that no child care will be provided. If you have young and squirmy ones please be prepared to step out from the ceremony if necessary.

Q: What is the weather at that time of year?

A: The first weekend in October typically marks the start of peak fall foliage season in New Hampshire. Temperatures are likely to be mid 50s - high 60s during the day and drop into the 40s at night. We're hoping for sunshine and crisp leaf-peeping weather, but colder temps and rain are a possibility as well. Bring a your favorite sweater and a couple jackets and we'll have a blast! As Tony likes to say: there's no such thing as bad weather - only bad gear.
